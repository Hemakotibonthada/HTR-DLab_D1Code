Cosmic Questions

  • Are we alone in the universe?

    With billions of galaxies each containing billions of stars, the mathematical probability suggests we may not be alone.

  • What existed before the Big Bang?

    This question may be meaningless, as time itself began with the Big Bang. Some theories suggest other universes, quantum fluctuations, or eternal inflation.

  • Is consciousness fundamental to reality?

    Some theories suggest consciousness may be as fundamental as space and time, while others view it as an emergent property of complex systems.

  • What is the ultimate fate of the universe?

    Current evidence points to an ever-expanding universe that will eventually reach "heat death" - a state of maximum entropy.

  • Do we live in a simulation?

    Some philosophers and scientists argue that advanced civilizations could create simulated realities indistinguishable from our own.
